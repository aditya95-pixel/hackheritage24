Political Science Career Path

About Political Science

Political Science is the study of government systems, political activity, and political behavior. It examines the theory and practice of politics, the analysis of political systems, and the behavior of political entities.

Career Opportunities

  • Political Analyst
    Analyzing political systems and trends.
  • Policy Advisor
    Advising governments on policy formulation.
  • Legislative Assistant
    Assisting lawmakers in drafting and evaluating legislation.
  • Public Relations Specialist
    Managing public communications for political entities.
  • Campaign Manager
    Leading political campaigns for elections.
