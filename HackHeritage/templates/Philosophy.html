Branches of Philosophy

Metaphysics

Metaphysics is the branch of philosophy that explores the nature of reality, existence, and the universe. It delves into questions such as: What is existence? What is the nature of objects and their properties?

Epistemology

Epistemology is the study of knowledge and belief. It investigates the nature of knowledge, the rationality of belief, and what distinguishes justified belief from opinion.

Ethics

Ethics, also known as moral philosophy, involves systematizing, defending, and recommending concepts of right and wrong behavior. It explores questions of how one ought to act and live.

Logic

Logic is the systematic study of the principles of valid inference and correct reasoning. It includes the evaluation of arguments and the development of criteria to assess the validity of reasoning.

Aesthetics

Aesthetics is the branch of philosophy that deals with the nature and appreciation of art, beauty, and taste. It seeks to understand how art is experienced and what makes something beautiful.
