Genres of Literature

Fiction

Fiction literature includes stories that are created from the imagination, often involving invented characters and events. Famous works of fiction include novels, short stories, and novellas.

Poetry

Poetry is a form of literary expression that uses rhythmic and often figurative language. It typically relies on meter, rhyme, and evocative word choice to convey emotions and ideas.

Drama

Drama literature focuses on plays and theatrical works. It is written with the intention of being performed, and it includes famous works ranging from tragedies to comedies.

Non-Fiction

Non-fiction literature is based on factual information and real events. This genre includes biographies, essays, journals, and historical works.

Folklore and Mythology

Folklore and mythology encompass traditional stories, customs, and legends passed down through generations. These stories often explain natural phenomena or cultural beliefs.
