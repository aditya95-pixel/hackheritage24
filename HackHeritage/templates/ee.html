Electrical Engineering

Overview

Electrical Engineering is a field of engineering that deals with the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. This discipline involves designing and developing electrical systems, equipment, and devices for a variety of applications.

Power Systems

Power Systems Engineering focuses on the generation, transmission, distribution, and utilization of electric power. It plays a key role in the design and management of electrical grids and renewable energy sources.

Control Systems

Control Systems Engineering involves the design and analysis of controllers that manage and regulate the behavior of dynamic systems. It is essential in applications such as automation, robotics, and aerospace.

Electronics

Electronics Engineering is concerned with the design and development of electronic circuits, devices, and systems. It includes areas like analog and digital circuit design, semiconductor devices, and integrated circuits.

Signal Processing

Signal Processing involves the analysis, interpretation, and manipulation of signals. It is used in applications such as communication systems, image processing, and audio engineering.

Instrumentation

Instrumentation Engineering focuses on the design and development of devices that measure and control physical quantities like temperature, pressure, and flow. It is crucial in industries such as manufacturing and healthcare.
