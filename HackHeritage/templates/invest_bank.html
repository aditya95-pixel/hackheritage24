Investment Banking Career Path Guidance

Career Opportunities in Investment Banking

Explore key areas in investment banking and associated career opportunities:

Capital Markets

Helping clients raise capital through equity and debt offerings.

  • Equity Analyst
    Research and analyze stocks and sectors to provide recommendations.
  • Debt Analyst
    Specializing in bonds and debt-raising strategies.

Advisory Services

Guiding clients on mergers, acquisitions, and financial transactions.

  • M&A Advisor
    Advising on mergers, acquisitions, and valuations.
  • Corporate Finance Consultant
    Advising on capital allocation and restructuring.
