Chemistry Career Path Guidance

Career Opportunities in Chemistry

Explore key areas in Chemistry and associated career opportunities:

Analytical Chemistry

Focusing on analyzing substances to determine their composition and concentration.

  • Analytical Chemist
    Using scientific techniques to identify the chemical makeup of substances.
  • Laboratory Technician
    Assisting in the collection and analysis of samples in a lab environment.

Organic Chemistry

Specializing in the structure, properties, and reactions of organic compounds.

  • Organic Chemist
    Working on the design and synthesis of organic molecules for pharmaceuticals, plastics, and more.
  • Medicinal Chemist
    Researching and developing new drugs by manipulating organic compounds.

Physical Chemistry

Investigating how matter behaves on a molecular and atomic level, and how chemical reactions occur.

  • Physical Chemist
    Studying the physical properties of molecules and atoms to develop new materials and products.
  • Materials Scientist
    Creating and improving materials by understanding their chemical properties.

Biochemistry

Exploring the chemical processes within and related to living organisms.

  • Biochemist
    Studying biological molecules and processes to develop new products, medicines, and therapies.
  • Toxicologist
    Assessing the effects of chemicals on living organisms to ensure safety and efficacy.
