Vocational Courses - ITI, Polytechnic, Vocational Training

Explore Vocational Courses

Vocational education provides practical skills and knowledge to prepare individuals for specific careers. Here are some key areas in vocational training:

Industrial Training Institute (ITI)

ITIs offer training in various trades such as electrician, plumber, and mechanic, equipping students with hands-on skills for industrial jobs.

  • Electrician
    Training in electrical installation, maintenance, and repair.
  • Plumber
    Skills in plumbing systems installation and maintenance.
  • Mechanic
    Learning mechanical repair and maintenance for various machines.

Polytechnic

Polytechnics offer diploma courses in engineering and technology fields, providing foundational knowledge for technical careers.

  • Civil Engineering Technician
    Assisting in the design and construction of infrastructure projects.
  • Mechanical Engineering Technician
    Supporting mechanical engineers in product design and development.
  • Electrical Engineering Technician
    Working on electrical systems design, testing, and maintenance.

Vocational Training Centers

These centers provide specialized training in various trades, enhancing employability in sectors such as hospitality, healthcare, and IT.

  • Hospitality Manager
    Training in hotel management, food services, and customer relations.
  • Healthcare Assistant
    Skills for supporting medical staff in patient care.
  • IT Support Specialist
    Training in technical support and IT infrastructure management.
