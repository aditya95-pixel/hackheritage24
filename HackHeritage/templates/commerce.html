Commerce Stream

Overview

The Commerce stream is a popular choice for students interested in business, trade, finance, and the economy. It opens up numerous career opportunities in accounting, business management, and economics.

Accounting

Accounting involves recording, classifying, and summarizing financial transactions to provide useful business decision information. Major areas include:

  • Financial Accounting
  • Cost Accounting
  • Management Accounting
  • Auditing
  • Taxation

Business Management

Business management involves planning, organizing, and controlling the operations of a business to achieve goals. Key areas include:

  • Human Resource Management
  • Operations Management
  • Marketing Management
  • Financial Management
  • Strategic Management

Economics

Economics is the study of how individuals and businesses make choices about resources. It includes:

  • Microeconomics
  • Macroeconomics
  • Development Economics
  • International Economics
  • Behavioral Economics

Finance

Finance focuses on the management of money and investments. Major areas include:

  • Corporate Finance
  • Investment Banking
  • Personal Finance
  • Financial Planning
  • Risk Management
