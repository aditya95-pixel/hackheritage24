Commerce Stream

Overview

The Commerce stream is a popular choice for students interested in the business, trade, finance, and economy. It opens up numerous career opportunities in various fields such as accounting, business management, economics, and finance.

Accounting

Accounting involves recording, classifying, and summarizing financial transactions to provide information that is useful in making business decisions. Major areas include:

  • Financial Accounting
  • Cost Accounting
  • Management Accounting
  • Auditing
  • Taxation

Business Management

Business Management involves planning, organizing, directing, and controlling the operations of a business to achieve organizational goals. Key areas include:

  • Human Resource Management
  • Operations Management
  • Marketing Management
  • Financial Management
  • Strategic Management

Economics

Economics is the study of how individuals, businesses, governments, and societies make choices about allocating resources. It includes areas such as:

  • Microeconomics
  • Macroeconomics
  • Development Economics
  • International Economics
  • Behavioral Economics

Finance

Finance is concerned with the management of money, investments, and other financial instruments. Major areas include:

  • Corporate Finance
  • Investment Banking
  • Personal Finance
  • Financial Planning
  • Risk Management
