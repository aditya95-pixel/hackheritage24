Arts Stream

Overview

The Arts stream offers a broad range of subjects and is ideal for students interested in creative, cultural, and social disciplines. It opens up a wide array of career options in fields such as humanities, social sciences, and performing arts.

Humanities

Humanities explore the human condition through subjects like history, literature, and philosophy. Major areas include:

  • History
  • Philosophy
  • Literature
  • Languages
  • Cultural Studies

Social Sciences

Social Sciences study society and human behavior. They include disciplines such as:

  • Psychology
  • Sociology
  • Political Science
  • Anthropology
  • Geography

Performing Arts

Performing Arts involve artistic expression through performance. Key areas include:

  • Theater
  • Music
  • Dance
  • Film and Television
  • Drama

Fine Arts

Fine Arts focus on visual arts such as painting, sculpture, and design. Important areas include:

  • Painting
  • Sculpture
  • Graphic Design
  • Photography
  • Illustration
