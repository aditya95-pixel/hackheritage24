Arts Stream

Overview

The Arts stream provides a broad range of disciplines for students interested in humanities, social sciences, and creative arts. It opens up a wide array of career options in fields such as Literature, History, Psychology, and Fine Arts.

Humanities

Humanities explore the human condition through subjects like history, literature, and philosophy. Major areas include:

  • History
  • Philosophy
  • Literature
  • Languages
  • Cultural Studies

Social Sciences

Social Sciences study society and human behavior. They include disciplines such as:

  • Psychology
  • Sociology
  • Political Science
  • Anthropology
  • Geography

Performing Arts

Performing Arts involve artistic expression through performance. Key areas include:

  • Theater
  • Music
  • Dance
  • Film and Television
  • Drama

Fine Arts

Fine Arts focus on visual arts such as painting, sculpture, and design. Important areas include:

  • Painting
  • Sculpture
  • Graphic Design
  • Photography
  • Illustration
