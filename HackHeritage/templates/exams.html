Entrance Examinations in India - Science, Commerce, Arts

Entrance Exams for Various Streams

India offers a range of entrance exams across Science, Commerce, and Arts streams. Below is an overview of key exams in each stream:

Science Stream

  • JEE Main & Advanced
    For admission to premier engineering institutes like IITs and NITs.
  • NEET
    For admission to medical courses like MBBS and BDS.
  • IISER Aptitude Test
    For admission to the Indian Institutes of Science Education and Research (IISERs).

Commerce Stream

  • CA Foundation
    Chartered Accountancy entry-level exam in India.
  • CS Executive Entrance Test (CSEET)
    For those aspiring to become Company Secretaries.
  • CMA Foundation
    For entry into Cost Management Accountancy.

Arts Stream

  • NID Entrance Exam
    For admission to National Institute of Design programs.
  • NIFT Entrance Exam
    For admission to National Institute of Fashion Technology programs.
  • CLAT
    For admission to undergraduate law courses in NLUs.
