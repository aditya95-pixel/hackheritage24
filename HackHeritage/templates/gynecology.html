Gynecology

Overview

The Gynecology Department focuses on the health of the female reproductive systems and the management of pregnancy and childbirth. This branch of medicine plays a critical role in the healthcare system, addressing a wide range of women's health issues.

Prenatal Care

Prenatal care involves regular check-ups and tests to ensure a healthy pregnancy and identify any potential complications early. Key areas include:

  • Ultrasound Monitoring
  • Genetic Testing
  • Nutrition and Lifestyle Guidance
  • Labor and Delivery Planning

Menstrual Health

Menstrual health management is crucial for the overall well-being of women. This includes addressing menstrual disorders, providing education, and promoting access to menstrual products. Key areas include:

  • Menstrual Disorders (e.g., PCOS, Menorrhagia)
  • Menstrual Hygiene Management
  • Hormonal Therapies
  • Menopause Management

Gynecologic Oncology

Gynecologic oncology specializes in diagnosing and treating cancers of the female reproductive system. Key areas include:

  • Cervical Cancer
  • Ovarian Cancer
  • Uterine Cancer
  • Vulvar and Vaginal Cancer

Reproductive Health

Reproductive health services include contraception, fertility treatments, and sexual health education. Key areas include:

  • Contraception and Family Planning
  • Fertility Treatments (IVF, IUI)
  • Sexually Transmitted Infections (STI) Management
  • Sexual Health Education
