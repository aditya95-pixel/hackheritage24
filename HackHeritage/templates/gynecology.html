Gynecology Department

Overview

The Gynecology Department focuses on the health of the female reproductive systems and the management of pregnancy and childbirth. This branch of medicine plays a critical role in the healthcare system, addressing a wide range of women's health issues.

Prenatal Care

Prenatal care involves regular check-ups and tests to ensure a healthy pregnancy and identify any potential complications early. Key areas include:

  • Ultrasound Monitoring

    Ultrasound Monitoring

    Ultrasound monitoring helps track the development of the fetus and detect any potential issues during pregnancy.

  • Genetic Testing

    Genetic Testing

    Genetic testing screens for genetic disorders that might affect the baby and helps in making informed decisions.

  • Nutrition and Lifestyle Guidance

    Nutrition and Lifestyle Guidance

    Guidance on maintaining a healthy diet and lifestyle to support a healthy pregnancy.

  • Labor and Delivery Planning

    Labor and Delivery Planning

    Planning for labor and delivery includes discussing preferences and preparing for childbirth.

Menstrual Health

Menstrual health management is crucial for the overall well-being of women. This includes addressing menstrual disorders, providing education, and promoting access to menstrual products. Key areas include:

  • Menstrual Disorders

    Menstrual Disorders

    Managing disorders like PCOS and Menorrhagia, which can affect menstrual regularity and health.

  • Menstrual Hygiene Management

    Menstrual Hygiene Management

    Providing education and resources for maintaining menstrual hygiene.

  • Hormonal Therapies

    Hormonal Therapies

    Using hormonal treatments to manage menstrual irregularities and disorders.

  • Menopause Management

    Menopause Management

    Support and treatment options for managing symptoms of menopause.

Gynecologic Oncology

Gynecologic oncology specializes in diagnosing and treating cancers of the female reproductive system. Key areas include:

  • Cervical Cancer

    Cervical Cancer

    Diagnosis and treatment of cancer affecting the cervix.

  • Ovarian Cancer

    Ovarian Cancer

    Diagnosis and treatment of cancer affecting the ovaries.

  • Uterine Cancer

    Uterine Cancer

    Diagnosis and treatment of cancer affecting the uterus.

  • Vulvar and Vaginal Cancer

    Vulvar and Vaginal Cancer

    Diagnosis and treatment of cancers affecting the vulva and vagina.

Reproductive Health

Reproductive health services include contraception, fertility treatments, and sexual health education. Key areas include:

  • Contraception and Family Planning

    Contraception and Family Planning

    Providing options for contraception and planning for family growth.

  • Fertility Treatments

    Fertility Treatments

    Treatments like IVF and IUI to assist with conception.

  • Sexually Transmitted Infections (STI) Management

    Sexually Transmitted Infections (STI) Management

    Diagnosis and treatment of STIs, and education on prevention.

  • Sexual Health Education

    Sexual Health Education

    Education on sexual health and safe practices.
