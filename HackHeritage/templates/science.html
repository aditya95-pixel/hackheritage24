Science Stream

Overview

The Science stream is a popular academic stream chosen by students who are interested in the natural world, technology, and scientific research. It opens up a wide range of career options in fields such as Engineering, Medicine, and Research.

Engineering

Engineering is a diverse field that applies scientific principles to design, build, and maintain structures, machines, devices, systems, and processes. It includes various branches such as:

  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering

Medicine

MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery) is the primary medical degree in many countries. It involves studying human health, diagnosing diseases, and treating patients. Major areas of specialization include:

  • General Medicine
  • Surgery
  • Pediatrics
  • Gynecology
  • Orthopedics

Research

Research in science is about exploring new ideas, experimenting, and advancing our understanding of the natural and physical world. Research can be conducted in various disciplines such as:

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Environmental Science
  • Mathematics
